The Interview

Demonstrate your skills in a mock interview with three rounds. Impress the interviewer and secure your spot!

For More Details
More Information
GENERAL RULES:

Imagine you are in a job interview with only 3 job vacancies. Do you have what it takes to persuade the interviewer to offer you the job? The interview will have 3 rounds:

  1. Round 1 - Self Introduction: Personality will be revealed during the event and preparation time will be given. Talk about the personality for a minute and justify why you deserve the job.
  2. Round 2 - Group Discussion: Groups will be split based on the number of participants. Talk about the same personality and comment on why other personalities do not deserve the job. Cross-questioning is allowed. Shortlisted teams will move to the Final round.
  3. Round 3 - The Interview: New personalities will be assigned. The interview will be conducted between two churches. Interviewer decision will be final.
TIME LIMIT:
Will be told on the spot

CREW:
1 participant per church.
